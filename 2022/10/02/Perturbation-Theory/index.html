Perturbation Theory

Non-degenerate Perturbation Theory

We can write the wave functions and corresponding energies:

\begin{align} \psi_{n} & =\psi_{n}^{(0)}+\lambda\psi_{n}^{(1)}+\lambda^2\psi_{n}^{(2)}+\cdots\\ E_{n} & =E_{n}^{(0)}+\lambda E_{n}^{(1)}+\lambda^2E_{n}^{(2)}+\cdots \end{align}

Energy Correction(1st Order)

E_{n}^{(1)}=\bra{\psi_{n}^{(0)}}H^{(1)}\ket{\psi_{n}^{(0)}}

Wave Equations(states) Correction(1st Order)

\psi_{n}^{(1)}=\sum_{m\neq n}\frac{\bra{\psi_{m}^{(0)}}H^{(1)}\ket{\psi_{n}^{(0)}}}{E_{n}^{(0)}-E_{m}^{(0)}}

Energy Correction(2nd Order)

[E_{n}^{(2)}]^2=\sum_{m(m\neq n)}\frac{\abs{\bra{\psi_{m}^{(0)}}H^{(1)}\ket{\psi_{n}^{(0)}}}^2}{E_{n}^{(0)}-E_{m}^{(0)}}
